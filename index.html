FAQ

Common questions

What file format do I need?

A standard CSV file. Export return data from Shopify, WooCommerce, your ERP, or any system — as long as it has columns for order ID, product name, and return reason, Returnly will handle the rest. We're flexible on column naming.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data is processed over HTTPS, used only to generate your analysis, and is never sold or shared. We do not store raw customer information beyond what's needed to render your report.

How long does analysis take?

Typically under 2 minutes for up to several hundred returns. The AI classification and insight generation run in parallel, so even large datasets come back fast.

What's included in the Pro Report?

The Pro Report is a branded, shareable PDF with your full analysis: executive summary, return category breakdown, sentiment analysis, top 10 problem SKUs with return values, and a prioritized action plan. It's designed to be presented to stakeholders or used as a working document.

Do I need any technical knowledge?

None at all. If you can export a spreadsheet from your store and upload a file, you can use Returnly. No integrations, no APIs, no setup. Upload a CSV, get your analysis.
